My Device Metrics

Understanding these device metrics helps you keep your system running smoothly by showing how resources are being used. Metrics like CPU cores, usage, and frequency tell you how powerful your processor is and if it's overworked. Disk usage and I/O show if your storage is full or if there are slow read/write speeds that could cause problems. Memory metrics show how much RAM is used or available, helping avoid slowdowns. The load average gives a snapshot of system activity, helping you spot potential issues early. Overall, these are good ways to understand the current "effort" of your device.
